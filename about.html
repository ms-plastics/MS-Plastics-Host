M S Plastics

Established in 1994 in Bengaluru, Karnataka, M.S. Plastics has been a leader in manufacturing and supplying high-quality battery containers, battery trolly cabinets, and battery accessories. With over Three decades of experience, we have built a solid reputation for delivering durable, efficient, and innovative solutions that meet the evolving demands of the industry.At M.S. Plastics, our mission is to provide cutting-edge products that set the standard in the battery manufacturing industry. We are committed to offering top-quality products, timely delivery, and unparalleled customer service to ensure the complete satisfaction of our clients.

What We Do

All of our products are manufactured using superior-grade raw materials and the latest technology, ensuring they meet rigorous industry standards. We specialize in the design and production of a wide range of battery containers and parts, including:

PP Containers

PP Containers are made from high-quality polypropylene, offering durability and heat resistance for battery applications. Our range includes standard and custom sizes designed to securely hold various battery types, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.

SEE ALL
Battery Trolly Cabinets

Battery Trolly Cabinets provide a reliable and mobile solution for storing and transporting heavy batteries. Designed for strength and stability, our cabinets feature corrosion-resistant finishes and smooth-rolling wheels, making them ideal for both industrial and residential use.

SEE ALL
Battery Accessories

Battery Accessories include essential components that enhance battery performance and reliability. Our extensive range features shims, supports, thermal caps, and floats, all crafted to ensure proper installation, stability, and maintenance of batteries across various applications.
