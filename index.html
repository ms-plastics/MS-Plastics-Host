M S Plastics

Established in the year 1994 at Bengaluru, (Karnataka, India), we, “M. S. Plastics,” are reckoned as one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of an extensive range of Heat Sealed Battery Containers and Battery Parts. Our experienced and qualified professionals, develop these products in strict compliance with the defined industry standards, using superior grade raw materials and ultra modern mechanical devices. We have earned specialization in providing Automobile Tubular Traction Battery Containers and Battery part. Available in numerous technical specifications, these products are appreciated for features like heat resistance, longer service life and durability.
